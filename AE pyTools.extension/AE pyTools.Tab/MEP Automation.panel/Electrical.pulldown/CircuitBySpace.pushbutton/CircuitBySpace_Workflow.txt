Circuit By Space Workflow Guide
===============================

Purpose: Creates circuits by grouping lighting fixtures by panel, space
bucket, and class.

Inputs
------
- Lighting fixtures from selection or model scope.
- Panel lookup data.
- Optional Planet Fitness helper rules for space splitting.

What It Changes
---------------
- Creates electrical circuits.
- Applies circuit metadata/load naming to created systems.

Run Sequence
------------
1. Run command and choose optional client mode.
2. Tool gathers fixture circuit context and space data.
3. Items are grouped by panel + space + standard/emergency class.
4. Optional helper rules split groups by fixture type/min circuits.
5. Circuits are created, then circuit data is applied.

Validation Checklist
--------------------
- Circuit totals match expected space grouping.
- Standard/emergency split is correct.
- Panel and load naming follow standards.

Troubleshooting
---------------
- No fixtures found: verify scope and category.
- Missing panel context: inspect circuit source parameters.
- Unexpected split: check helper rules and space naming.
